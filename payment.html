Bug Bounty Programs

Private & Public BBP Terms

Specialized payment structure for bug bounty programs. Fair for companies, fair for researchers.

For Companies / Clients

  • Validation Period: EzekielSec validates each vulnerability within 7 business days
  • Payment Due: Within 30 calendar days after validation confirmation
  • Late Payment: 10% late fee applied after 30 days; program paused until payment received
  • Dispute Resolution: Must be raised within 14 days of validation
30 days to pay after validation

For Security Researchers

  • Bounty Payment: Paid within 45 days after client payment is received by EzekielSec
  • Remediation Hold: Must wait until remediation is complete before public disclosure
  • Embargo Period: Standard 90-day coordinated disclosure window
  • Good Faith: No payment for duplicates, out-of-scope, or already known issues
Be patient — paid after client settles

Important Note for Researchers

EzekielSec pays bounties after receiving client payment. Researchers are expected to be patient and cooperate during the remediation phase. No public disclosure until remediation is confirmed and the embargo period ends. This ensures responsible disclosure and protects all parties involved.
